Spaghetti

Description

Who doesn't love an easy-to-make yet tasteful, Italian dish? With our Spaghetti Bolognese recipe you'll wade yourselves at a trattoria in Roma right from within your own kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 250g wholegrain pasta
  • 5 tomatoes
  • 300g minced meat
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian spices
  • Optional: parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water with some salt for 8 minutes
  2. Fry minced meat in frying pan on medium heat until light-brown with salt, pepper
  3. Dice the onion and add to the minced meat. Stir fry until golden
  4. Add 1tsp of italian spices
